Legal Services Across the Matters That Matter Most

Amar Legal offers dependable representation and advice across a broad range of practice areas. Whatever your situation, our team brings focused expertise and genuine commitment to your cause.

01 — Civil Law

Civil Litigation & Disputes

We represent clients in a wide spectrum of civil matters — property disputes, breach of contract, recovery suits, tort claims and civil appeals. Our approach combines careful preparation with sound courtroom strategy to protect your rights and interests.

  • Property & title disputes
  • Contract & recovery matters
  • Civil appeals & revisions
02 — Corporate

Corporate & Commercial Law

From company formation to ongoing compliance, we provide practical legal solutions for businesses of every size. Our services cover corporate governance, commercial contracts, regulatory matters and dispute resolution — helping you operate with confidence.

  • Company formation & compliance
  • Commercial contracts & agreements
  • Corporate advisory & governance
03 — Family

Family & Matrimonial Law

Family matters demand both legal skill and human sensitivity. We handle divorce, maintenance, child custody, adoption and domestic disputes with discretion and compassion — always mindful of the emotional weight these matters carry.

  • Divorce & mutual settlements
  • Maintenance & custody
  • Adoption & guardianship
